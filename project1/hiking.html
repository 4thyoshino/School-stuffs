Ice climbing & hiking on glaciers

  

glaciers Experience is a small group experience that offers an easy glaciers hike on Solheimajokull glacier with an experienced glaciers guide and is available all year around. The tour offers a pick-up from Reykjavik or a meet on location at Solheimajokull glacier. Add even more adventure to your stay in Iceland and come explore this amazing crawling glacier with us.

Solheimajokull glaciers is an outlet from the might Myrdalsjokull and is surrounded by some of Iceland's most grand volcanoes and during summertime, a glacier lagoon forms in front of the glacier only adding to the charm of the location. This popular day tour is the perfect way to get in real contact with the true reason behind the name Ice-land!
